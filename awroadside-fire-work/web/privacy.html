Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

AW Roadside ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, request roadside assistance, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email, phone number, and location data when requesting services.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including connecting you with roadside assistance providers.

3. Location Services

Our app requires access to your location to accurately dispatch roadside assistance. This information is shared with providers only when you request a service.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@awobemedia.com.
